South Indian Meals

South Indian meals are known for their diverse flavors, reliance on fresh ingredients, and traditional cooking methods, with rice being a staple. They are characterized by the use of aromatic spices, vegetables, and variety of chutneys and curries, tamarind, curry leaves, and mustard seeds.


Pancake

A pancake is a flat, round cake made from a batter of flour, eggs, and milk, typically fried on both sides on a griddle or in a frying pan. It's a popular breakfast dish, often served with butter and syrup, but can also be enjoyed with a variety of toppings and fillings, both sweet and savory.


Fast Foods

Burgers, french fries, and Coca-Cola are popular fast-food items, often associated with each other. Burgers are typically a patty of ground meat (often beef) served in a bun, often with toppings like lettuce, tomato, and condiments. French fries are deep-fried potatoes, usually cut into strips.
